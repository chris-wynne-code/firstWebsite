Being adept in both Premiere Pro and Final Cut Pro, I use both interchangeably depending on the task at hand.
Generally, I use Premiere Pro for editing professional work - especially captioning content - as it is considered to be industry standard software at the moment.
I practice using Final Cut Pro on personal projects.
In addition to this, I often will create/edit assets to be utilised in videos using Photoshop or Adobe After Effects, along with sourcing content online like music or images - copyright free if it is for professional work - in order to elevate the presentation.
